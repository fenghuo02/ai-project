## State of the Empire

### 🚗 Tesla

q1 2026 deliveries came in at 358,023. that's a miss against ~365k consensus, with another 50k stuck in inventory. the headlines wrote themselves.

but the buried number is fsd subscriptions. 1.28M as of q1, up 51% YoY from 850k a year ago. that's now ~14% of the active fleet running fsd. 2 years ago bears said it'd never get past 5%.

fsd v14.3.3 is shipping. cybercab pilot fleet in texas hit 29 cars, with 70+ at giga texas observed by may 13.

stock dropped under $400 on may 19 — model y price increase reaction.

**next 90 days**: q2 deliveries (early july), fsd v14.4, optimus consumer launch end-2027.

### 🚀 SpaceX

starship V3 had its first flight may 22. specs are wild: 408ft tall, 100 metric ton payload (vs 35t on v2), 33× raptor 3 engines, 18M lbs of thrust.

s-1 filed with the SEC on may 20. ipo target $1.75-$2T, raising ~$75B. expected next month.

2025 ebitda came in at $6.6B per the s-1 — first time spacex disclosed real numbers publicly. tam claim: $28.5T.

**next 90 days**: ipo pricing range, in-orbit refuel test, starship reuse cadence.

**moat ppl forget**: nothing else fully reusable at scale. nothing close.

### 🛰️ Starlink

10M subscribers as of feb 2026. for context: 4M sep 2024 → 9M dec 2025 → 10M feb 2026. fastest-growing internet service in history.

~10,370 active satellites in orbit. target constellation is 42,000.

**next 90 days**: v2 batch deployment, direct-to-cell expansion, enterprise/maritime push.

**watch**: amazon kuiper rollout. starlink ARPU pressure.

### 🤖 xAI (now part of SpaceXAI)

elon confirmed sunday (may 25) that grok V9-Medium completed training. 1.5 trillion parameters. public release in 2-3 weeks. grok 4.2 will be open-sourced by EOY.

colossus is at 555,000 GPUs with a 2GW power target. that's $18B in GPU investment, the largest AI training cluster on earth.

the 2025 numbers from spacex's s-1: $6.4B operating loss on $3.2B revenue. capex $12.7B. q1 2026 capex alone $7.7B (annualized $30B+).

**next 90 days**: V9-Medium public release, grok 4.2 open-source drop, orbital compute infrastructure planning.

**moat ppl forget**: x is the only AI lab with a real-time human reasoning corpus across 200 countries. h100 stacks alone can't buy that.

### 🐦 X (platform)

valuation back to ~$44B (the acquisition price). 2025 ad revenue $1.8B, up 7% YoY but still 59% below the 2021 peak. US digital ad market share at ~1% (was ~10% in 2016).

@grok summons inline = quietly the best feature shipped on this platform in 2 years.

**next 90 days**: native creator scheduling, deeper xai training pipeline integration.

### 🧠 Neuralink

21 participants enrolled as of january. that's the milestone most still don't know.

series E-II closed may 6, $650M raise. valuation now $9.7B. backers: ARK, Founders Fund, Sequoia, Thrive, Lightspeed.

**next 90 days**: 2nd clinical site approval, bps decoder doubling, speech decoding.

**different game**: gating factor here is fda not engineering. fda breakthrough device path was locked in 2023, compresses approval ~3 years.

### 🚇 Boring Co

valuation ~$5.7B, down from $8.6B 2023 peak. that's the underreported part of the empire.

vegas loop: 3M+ cumulative passengers since opening, expanded to 2.1 miles.

nashville music city loop: $34M tunnel deal with BNA airport, 40-year licensing term, projected $300M+ rider fees.

3 cities picked for tunnels in march 2026.

**watching**: nevada legislative probe over safety/environmental violations. negative catalyst risk.

**next 90 days**: nashville construction start, prufrock-4 unveil, 2nd-city contract.
