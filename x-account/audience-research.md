# Audience Research Playbook · 季度 survey + 月度 1:1 + Cohort 追踪

> 状态：v1.0 · 2026-05-30
> 触发：v3+v4 没有 feedback loop = 拍脑袋决策。Top creator 2026 行业实践都做季度 reader survey + 月度 5 个 1:1 interview。
> 配套：[personal-crm.md](./personal-crm.md) · [sustainability-and-delegation.md](./sustainability-and-delegation.md) · [_self-operation-handbook.md](./_self-operation-handbook.md)

## 一、为什么必须建立 feedback loop

### 1.1 没 feedback 的代价

```
现状（v3+v4 默认）:
  · 你写内容 → 数据反馈
  · 数据高 → 加大
  · 数据低 → 减少
  · 拍脑袋决策（哪些 series 砍掉 / 改频率 / 哪些主题深挖）

问题:
  · 数据反映"算法 + 时机"，不是"受众真实需求"
  · 高 imp 不代表受众真喜欢（点击 ≠ value）
  · 你在 echo chamber（只看自己 timeline）
  · 6 个月后才发现"原来受众想要 X"
```

### 1.2 行业证据（2026-05 联网验证）

- [SparkToro 2025/2026 audience research 完整指南](https://sparktoro.com/blog/audience-research-the-complete-guide-for-marketers/)：top creator 季度 reader survey 是必备 infrastructure
- [Backlinko audience research](https://backlinko.com/audience-research)：marketers 太常 equate audience research with half-baked CRM data
- [Automateed audience interviews](https://www.automateed.com/how-to-run-audience-interviews-as-a-creator)：5 个深度 interview 比 50 个 survey 有价值
- [Wynter audience research](https://wynter.com/post/audience-research)：primary research 是 creator 不可替代的优势

> 内容已合规化转述。

### 1.3 你目前阶段（M1）不做的代价

```
M1 (now) 不做：感觉良好继续走
M2 不做：5K 粉发现 60% 是来自 musk 主题，但 v2 brand 已切 observer
M3 不做：12K 粉但 retention 38%（行业 base 75%），来不及挽回
M4 不做：30K 粉但 paid conversion 0.5%（行业 base 2%），收入卡

vs 做：
M1 做：第 1 次 survey N=15-30
M2 做：第 2 次 survey N=50-100，5 个 1:1 interview
M3 做：第 3 次 survey N=200，复制 1:1
M4 做：dashboard 已成熟 + churn 模型 + 内容 calendar 数据驱动
```

## 二、3 个研究方法（深度 + 广度组合）

### 2.1 Method A · 季度 Reader Survey (10-15 题)

```
频率: 每季度 1 次 (1, 4, 7, 10 月)
形式: Tally / Google Forms
长度: 10-15 题, 5 min 完成
受众: Beehiiv subscriber + X follower
回收预期 (M2 起):
  N = subscribers × 5-10%
  M2: 100 subs → 5-10 responses
  M3: 1K subs → 50-100 responses
  M5: 5K subs → 250-500 responses

价值: quantitative pattern, broad signal
```

### 2.2 Method B · 月度 1:1 Interview (5 人)

```
频率: 每月 1 次, 5 个 30-min interview
形式: Calendly + Zoom / Spaces
受众: S Tier + A Tier from [personal-crm.md](./personal-crm.md)
长度: 30 min depth conversation

价值: qualitative depth, "why" behind data
```

### 2.3 Method C · Cohort Tracking

```
频率: 持续 (Beehiiv + Notion 自动化)
形式: 每个新 sub / new follower 来自哪 source
追踪: 30 / 60 / 90 / 180 天 retention rate

价值: identify 哪个内容来源带来 high LTV fans
```

## 三、Method A · Quarterly Reader Survey

### 3.1 启动时机

```
M1 现在 (Beehiiv 50+ subs 后第一时间)
  → 第 1 次 survey: 简化版 5 题
M2 (Beehiiv 200+ subs)
  → 第 2 次 survey: 完整 10 题
M3 起每季度: 完整 15 题
```

### 3.2 第 1 次（M1 简化）5 题

```
[Tally form: Reader Survey · M1 Edition]

1. Why did you subscribe? (multi-choice)
   □ musk empire tracking
   □ AI race coverage
   □ markets / IPO observation
   □ humor pillars
   □ cultural observation
   □ other: [open text]

2. What's the 1 thing you'd want more of?
   [open text, 50 char min]

3. What's the 1 thing you'd want less of?
   [open text, 50 char min]

4. How many minutes / week do you spend reading my content?
   □ < 5 min
   □ 5-15 min
   □ 15-30 min
   □ 30-60 min
   □ > 60 min

5. Would you pay $10/month for premium?
   □ definitely
   □ probably
   □ maybe
   □ no
```

### 3.4 SOP 流程 (季度初 Day 1)

```
Day 1 (季度第 1 周一):
  · 在 Beehiiv newsletter 发 survey link
  · X 主推一条 "新季度 reader survey, 5 min, [link]"
  · X bio 加 short link "→ tell me what you want next quarter"

Day 1-7:
  · 每天 evening reply 已答的人 (acknowledge)
  · 每 100 个答 → 1 个 "lucky 1:1 / cheat sheet" reward (生成 attention)

Day 8 (1 周后):
  · 关闭 survey
  · 数据 export
  · 分析 (3-4 hr · 见 §3.5)

Day 14 (2 周后):
  · 发 results post: "what 100 of you said"
  · 把 1-2 个 actionable changes 公开 commit
  · 增加 reader trust (让他们看到 feedback 被听到)

Day 14+:
  · Update [_self-operation-handbook.md] §一 调整内容比例
  · Update [signature-series.md] 调整 series 重点
  · Update [revenue-stack-2026.md] 价格 / 产品方向
```

## 四、Method B · Monthly 1:1 Interviews (5 人)

### 4.1 选谁

```
S Tier (top 10 from personal CRM): 
  · Always include 1-2 in monthly interview pool

A Tier (top 50):
  · 2-3 per month rotation

Special invites:
  · Just-bookmarked-3-times-this-week 受众 (high signal)
  · Recent Founding Member candidates
  · Diverse backgrounds (1 tech, 1 finance, 1 operator etc)
```

### 4.2 Outreach DM

```
hey [first name],

quick + specific.

i'm running 5 reader interviews this month to figure out
what's actually useful vs what's noise.

30 min, your schedule: [Calendly link]
i'll send you a $25 amazon gift card after.

if interested, click. if not, no follow-up.

— @Skub0k
```

5 邀请通常 5-10 接受。月度 5 完成。

### 4.3 30-min Interview Agenda

```
[Run via Spaces or Zoom recording, ask consent first]

0-3 min · setup
  · "thanks for joining. today is just learning, no sell."
  · "you'll get $25 gift card after"
  · "i'll record only audio, not video, only I see"

3-15 min · their context
  · How did you find me?
  · How long subscribed?
  · What did you read this morning?
  · How do you decide what to read?
  · 把 interview 设计成 "tell me about your week" 不是 "tell me about me"

15-25 min · my content
  · What did you bookmark recently from anyone (not just me)?
    → [reveals real value model]
  · If you had to cut my content to 1 thing per week, what?
    → [reveals priority]
  · What 1 thing do you wish i covered but i don't?
    → [reveals gap]
  · What's the 1 thing that would make you cancel subscription?
    → [reveals risk]

25-30 min · close
  · "anything you want to ask me?"
  · "would you be open to a 6-month follow-up?"
  · gift card sent

After:
  · 30 min summary write-up (Notion)
  · Identify themes across 5 interviews 月度
```

### 4.4 不要做的

```
❌ Sell during interview (背叛 trust)
❌ Defend criticism (just listen)
❌ Push survey questions instead of open conversation
❌ Skip recording or not get consent
❌ Use participants' name publicly without permission
❌ Group interviews (different dynamics)
```

## 五、Method C · Cohort Tracking

### 5.1 数据 schema

```
[Notion Database: Subscriber Cohorts]

Properties:
  · Email (text)
  · Subscribe date
  · Source (X / Reading List / Boost / Substack / Bluesky / Direct)
  · Tier (S/A/B/C/D from CRM)
  · Engagement score (0-100, 自动 by activity)
  · 30-day retention (Y/N)
  · 90-day retention
  · 180-day retention
  · LTV (cumulative paid amount)
  · Notes
```

### 5.2 关键 metric

```
Source quality matrix:

| Source       | sub count | 30d ret | 90d ret | LTV mean |
|--------------|-----------|---------|---------|----------|
| Direct X     |           |         |         |          |
| Reading List |           |         |         |          |
| Boost        |           |         |         |          |
| Substack     |           |         |         |          |
| Bluesky      |           |         |         |          |

Insight: 哪个 source 最高 LTV → 加大投入
       哪个 source 最高 churn → 减少投入
```

### 5.3 月度 cohort review

```
每月最后一天加 30 min:
  □ Update Notion database with last month subs
  □ Compute matrix
  □ Identify top quality source
  □ Identify worst quality source (likely paid Boost)
  □ Adjust acquisition budget
  □ Note in monthly retro
```

### 5.4 自动化（M3+）

```
M3 起用 SparkToro / Beehiiv API 自动化:
  · UTM tracking on all signup links
  · Auto-categorize source
  · Auto-compute retention rate
  · Slack / email notify weekly
  · ~$30-50/月

M5 起加 PostHog (event analytics):
  · 内容互动事件
  · 用户行为模型
  · Cohort 漏斗模型
```

## 六、把 feedback 转成行动 SOP

### 6.1 季度 survey → action items

```
1. Survey 关 N 天后:
   · 阅读所有 open-text 答案 (1-2 hr)
   · 标 4 类 themes
   · 写 ACTION ITEMS list

2. Top 3 actions ←
   · 1 个 series 调整 (砍 / 改 / 加)
   · 1 个 voice 调整 (更专 / 更深 / 更广)
   · 1 个 product 调整 (price / feature / packaging)

3. 1 周内执行:
   · Update relevant docs
   · 给 readers commit (newsletter post)
   · Track 哪 action items 做了 / 没做
```

### 6.2 月度 5 interview → action items

```
1. Each interview after:
   · 30 min 写 summary
   · 标 1 个 surprising thing
   · 标 1 个 confirming thing

2. 5 个 interview 后 (月底):
   · Cross-reference themes
   · 哪些重复出现?
   · 哪些 outlier (一个人独有)?

3. 决定 actions:
   · 重复主题 = 立刻 action
   · Outlier = 备注但不 action

4. 在 newsletter / X 偶尔 reference:
   · "5 readers said [X] this month, so I'm adjusting [Y]"
   · 受众感觉 "我被听到"
```

### 6.3 连续观察 cohort retention

```
观察:
  · 哪个 cohort (按 source / 月份) 30/90/180 day retention 高?
  · LTV per cohort?
  · Churn pattern?

Action:
  · High-retention source = 加大投入
  · Low-retention source = 减少
  · Surprising churn (S Tier 走) = 立刻 1:1 救
  · Cohort effect (某月 sub 都流失) = 复盘那月做了什么
```

## 八、Privacy + Ethics

```
✅ 永远 anonymize 在公开内容
✅ 1:1 interview 永远 paid (gift card)
✅ Survey responses 永远 aggregated 在公开
✅ 不卖 / 不分享受众 list

❌ 公开 individual responses without permission
❌ 联系 survey 拒答的人施压
❌ 给 1:1 interview 受众施压买产品
❌ Use 数据训练 AI 卖
❌ Use 数据 spam mailing list
```

## 十一、5 件不要做

```
1. ❌ 太早 over-research（M1 N=10 不值得花 10 hr 分析）
2. ❌ 听任 1 个反对意见就改方向（cherry-pick 风险）
3. ❌ 因为 survey 受众 want X 就立刻给 X（受众"want" ≠ "need" / will pay）
4. ❌ Public 抱怨数据低（信任崩）
5. ❌ Use research as procrastination（"I need more data" 是不行动的借口）
```
